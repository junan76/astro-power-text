The writing workflow should stay file-based. Regular posts live under `src/content/posts`, while project articles live under the matching project directory.

Use short filenames that can become stable URLs:

- `responsive-reading-layouts.md`
- `github-pages-deployment.md`
- `search-index-design.md`

Frontmatter should describe the page clearly enough for lists, RSS, and SEO metadata.
